This is a language guessing game. Type in a phrase and choose a level. There are 5 regular levels and 6 special regional levels that you have to unlock. The phrase that you typed will be translated into a different language based on the level you chose. You must then guess the language it has been translated into from a list of 4 choices. If you get it right, the phrase will be translated into a different language from the same language level. Once you get ten right in a level, you go up to the next level. After you win level 5 the language regions are unlocked. If you guess incorrectly, you must start again.
